The Ballad of Digital Times

Based on "Cordel Moderno" — Milton Duarte

I'm getting tired of this show
Technology everywhere I go
People don't talk face to face
They text in a digital space.
Twitter, TikTok, Instagram fame
Always running the same game
Hearts and likes replace a smile.

Emails colder than the night
Chats that vanish out of sight
Conversations short and fast
Moments too quick to ever last
Selfies posted every day
Real life slipping far away
Silence louder than before.

Children don't run in the street
No ball to kick, no friends to meet
They'd rather stay behind the screen
Living in a glowing machine
Old guitar collects the dust
Stories fading into rust
Tradition drowned in neon light.

But deep inside the soul remains
Old roots stronger than the chains
Voices rise with simple song
To remind where we belong
Though the world may digitize
Culture never truly dies
It survives in heart and verse.
